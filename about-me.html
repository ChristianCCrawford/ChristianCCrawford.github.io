Christian Crawford

Engineer, Designer, Game Developer, and more

Hello, my name is Christian Crawford. I'm a programmer currently studying Computer Information Systems at Hampton University. I developed my love for technology from a young age and it has only grown stronger as I continued to learn more throughout the years. This passion lead me to take courses in web design and development and Java programming in high school. As well as pursue and participate in hackathons, personal programming and design projects, pitch competitions, and a software engineering bootcamp.

Entering into middle school I was chosen as a candidate to attend the Junior National Young Leaders Conference (JrNYLC), the most prestigious middle school leadership development program in the United States. This conference was a one week experience taking place in Washington, D.C. During this period I learned about the history of Washington, D.C. and developed my professional and critical thinking skills through various projects. Also within middle school, I participated in Robohullabaloo, a sumo Lego robotics workshop and competition where I learned to design, construct, and battle sumo robots using Lego Mindstorms NXT 2.0 programming. After completing high school with a 3.5 GPA, I was selected and attended the Computer Science Pre-College program at Hampton University which was sponsored by Google. During the program I was taught by Google engineers the foundations of Computer Science and exposed to languages and technology such as Python, HTML, CSS, Sublime Text, and many more.

During the academic school year of 2020-2021, I participated in a pitch competition program hosted by Black Venture Capital Consortium, a nonprofit organization aimed at closing the racial wealth gap between minorities in particular, African Americans and Caucasians. During this program I was taught necessary software engineering and user experience skills to tackle my pitch project, Study Sesh. For the Summer of 2021, I participated in BVCC software engineering bootcamp. This was a two-week software engineering boot camp which I learned learned topics and software such as Git, Jupyter Notebooks, machine learning, data science, and then completed an automation application within this time period. Shortly after my software engineering bootcamp I started my software engineering internship at Target. During this internship I was exposed to different concepts, languages, tools, and a fast pace and welcoming work environment. This includes: Kotlin, Groovy, MongoDB, Grafana, unit testing, Typescript, Jira, and many more. Currently, I am a UX lead at Hampton University, for BVCC and pursuing internship and other opportunities.

When I'm not absorbed in the digital world, I enjoy spending my time watching anime, youtube, playing a moderate amount of video games, and reading(especially manga). As well as, enjoying spending time with friends and family. Some of my favorite video games to play is Naruto Ninja Storm 4: Road To Boruto, .hack//GU Last Recode, Super Smash Bros Ultimate, and Sekiro. Some of my favorite anime and manga to read is One Piece, Jujutsu Kaisen, Black Clover, and Kingdom.

I am open and excited for future collaboration and creation with other software engineers, game developers and other creators. I love to hear about computer science related topics, gaming, anime, and anything else. So, please feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or shoot me an email!
