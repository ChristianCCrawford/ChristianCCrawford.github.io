Introduction

This website is as simple and straightforward as the title suggests. This was one of the very first personal projects that I worked on. During my sophomore year of college, I developed this website utilizing HTML and CSS. One of the purposes of this project is to gain more experience with HTML and CSS as well as have an accomplished project under my belt.

This page was supposed to be a brief and subtle introduction to my character and who I am. There are 5 main elements that define the structure of my website which are as follows: my name, profession/specialty, a detailed description of who I am, links to external sources, and the background image. At the time, I was highly interested in web design and web development. I am still interested, but I am also intrigued by other fields and specialties. Within the detailed description, I provided a brief overview of my major, the college I attend, and the languages I am proficient in to provide the user with an idea of my specialties and current skills. I am a huge lover of games, especially video games and arcades, so what could be better than choosing arcade games as the background image? So, when it came to choosing the background I wanted to display my interest while also displaying my personality. I believe this best encapsulates my interests as I tried to portray them.
